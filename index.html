About Me

Career

I am currently working at Expedia, Inc. as a Technical Product Manager on the Search Engine Marketing Engineering team. My goal was always to be a TPM, and I felt that in order to be empathetic and knowledgable about software developer processes, I should work as a Software Dev Engineering first. I started at Expedia in April 2017 as a Software Dev Engineer and after about 1.5 years, I felt comfortable to move to the TPM role. Expedia has such a wonderful culture and I really feel valued as an employee. I am learning a lot from my peers and hope to continue to grow in this role.

My experience in Software Development along with my previous experience as an Industrial Engineer has been a very interesting combination that makes me a unique addition to any team. I am motivated by a desire to enrich the community by contributing my strong communication and leadership skills, attention to detail, and ability to bring teams together.

My goal in every step of my career is to build products that will help or educate people on how to improve their lives and communities.

I am currently very happy in my role, but would love to chat with you about an innovative environment where I can offer my leadership, communication skills, and attention to detail.
